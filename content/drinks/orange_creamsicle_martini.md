## Ingredients: 
1 oz of Vodka
1 oz of Triple Sec
1 oz of Orange Juice
1 oz of Vanilla Ice Cream

## Directions:

* Mix together the vodka, triple sec, orange juice, and ice cream
* Shake well
* Serve

{{% notice note %}}
You can substitute half and half for the ice cream but if you do add some ice too
{{% /notice %}}
